Beyond Shelter, We Build Futures.

At Mojake, every child receives quality education, emotional support from dedicated house mothers, and access to healthcare. We believe in nurturing the whole child — mind, body and spirit — so they grow into leaders of Kisii.

Our holistic approach includes after-school tutoring, counseling, and regular medical camps.

Our Mission

To provide a loving, safe and nurturing home for orphans and vulnerable children in Kisii County, ensuring they have access to quality education, healthcare, and emotional support to break the cycle of poverty.

Our Vision

A future where every child in Kisii grows up with dignity, becomes self-reliant, and contributes positively to their community — rooted in Kenyan values and global opportunity.

Our Core Programs

Education

School fees, uniforms, and tutoring for every child.

Healthcare

Regular checkups, vaccinations, and nutrition support.

Emotional Care

Counseling, mentorship, and family-like bonds.

Nutrition

Three balanced meals daily from our kitchen.
